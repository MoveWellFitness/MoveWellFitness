About MoveWell

Our mission is to help you move better, feel stronger, and live healthier with personalized fitness coaching — wherever you are.

MoveWell was founded to bring expert personal training to busy people who want flexible, accessible, and effective fitness programs. Whether you're training in your living room, local park, or through video calls — we meet you where you are. We focus on realistic goals, sustainable habits, and empowering people of all fitness levels.

Led by certified trainers with a passion for coaching and movement, we believe fitness should be inclusive, enjoyable, and tailored to your lifestyle.

Our Services

Fitness solutions designed for real life — personalized, flexible, and effective.

1-on-1 Personal Training

We bring the workout to you. Whether you're training at home, in a park, or your building’s gym, our certified coaches tailor each session to your goals, ability, and space.

Online Coaching

Work with us remotely through custom programming, video tutorials, and weekly check-ins. Track your progress and stay consistent with support from real coaches.

Group & Partner Sessions

Motivate each other with affordable and energizing small group or partner sessions. Great for families, friends, and corporate wellness programs.

Specialized Programs

We also offer programs for pre/postnatal fitness, mobility and recovery, and sports-specific training. Ask us how we can help with your unique needs.
